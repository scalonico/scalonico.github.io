I am an econometrician whose research focuses on causal inference, nonparametric methods, and data analytics, with an emphasis on practical tools for empirical research. My work spans econometric theory, software for applied researchers, and collaborations in health and social policy, with the goal of making modern empirical methods both statistically rigorous and practically usable. Sebastian Calonico is Assistant Professor at the UC Davis Graduate School of Management, where his research connects econometric methods to applied problems in health, labor, and social policy.
